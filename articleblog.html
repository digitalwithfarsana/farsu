Every author flies in between their powerful words

Writing is a creative art in which an author execute thoughts to an ideal world far beyond the reality. Each time an Author born, a new word give birth to powerful sentences. This can be called the herculean connection between a writer and the alphabets

Article Writing is not a tough thing but a bit creative thing to be considered. Each writing holds different styles and presentation. Here I will share my great strategies which converts a reader to the ultimate writer. There are 8 major steps to be considered while moving with an article.

RESEARCH LIGHT UP YOUR BRAIN

Every intelligence come through a detailed study of a preferred topic (Search with a proper focus because all the search engines love traffic). Invest your much time here because good content bloom within a good research. Study it and enhance the true meaning by capturing the right information to your minds.

ASPIRE TO INSPIRE

Each word is an offering from the universe and though an Author must aspire to inspire the readers with creative ideology and thinking. A poetry must sing across your thoughts connecting to the selected topic. And now convert the poetry of your thoughts into inspiring heart touching words so you won in selecting the accurate thoughts and ideas.

SHAPE YOUR INFORMATION

The very next step is to shape or figure your knowledge. Try to write with a striking informative sentence targeting the audience. Put your creative thoughts and ideas on that particular topic and picture the writing to embrace the reader. In shaping your information and ideas you must attain certain attributes in writing
• Title must be catchy one
• Introduction should catch the audience
• Put Creative thoughts to bloom
• Add Keywords

CREATIVITY IN SPLITTING

Arrange the article to the perfect paragraphs. Each section should effectively detailed with its most accurate meaning. The reader must be able to catch your ideas and thoughts in a simple way. An article must contain information through dividing the sections. Each section should be ground breaking. There are some ways to group or split the topics to certain parts and they are,
• Introduction
• Present relevance
• Main body or part
• Cons and pros
• Conclusion

CONSTRUCT EFFECTIVE SENTENCES

As I said, sentences are born from words though construct effective sentences using high vocabularies and meaningful thoughts. Luxury languages can be used but it should never affect the simplicity of the article. It must be digestive and you should embrace a writer’s talents to build your article attractive and informative.

MAINTAIN BALANCE IN GRAMMAR

You must be concerned regarding the grammar that should be continued till closing sentence. Add proper grammar and you must proof read your article in considering the right rules.

EFFECTIVE CONCLUSION

As an effective introduction, conclusion also must be an eye catching one. Try to give a message or a quote on the preferred topic which will inspire a reader. Every writer’s goal is to make the readers smile, so give an inspiring conclusion that every reader can take it into their life. Life is precious, so give something worth.

PLAGIARISM CHECKING

After your completion, check your article plagiarised or not. You can use some plagiarism detecting tool to get the perfect analysis. Be aware that you can’t lie to search engines because they find the correct things and give the perfect results.

You can be a perfect author by achieving all these steps. Nothing can win through a simple way, each time we struggle we get new things. Embrace your talents, give yourself best because you are powerful enough to beat your competitors.

Be aware that ‘A true Writer reveals the perfect Reader’
