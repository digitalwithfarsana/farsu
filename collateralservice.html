Marketing collateral is any media material used to promote a company's products or services including any type of written material that helps market a business, product, service, or idea, such as flyers, information sheets, and brochures of all sizes, annual reports, and even folders that hold several of the pieces everything from print materials like posters and flyers to digital content like catalogues and e-magazines or anything you can use to communicate your company's brand message is considered marketing collateral. Marketing collateral content writing is explained as communication material sent out from a corporate to its existing and potential customers.
