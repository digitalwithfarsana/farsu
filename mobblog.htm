How mobile app development fared during the covid-19 outbreak

FROM AN OPTION TO THE NECESSITY THING

The pandemic outbreak has confined a big part of the population indoors. This has had a direct impact on mobile app usage trends, especially among on-demand mobile apps. Still the pandemic survives, humans survive through a Mobile phone where all the needed things are updated properly. The pandemic created a huge impact to the people around the world taking many lives. Every routine changed, everything created a fear in the people. The usage of mobile apps increased during the lockdown where all the humans where restricted to their freedom. People are using mobile apps for several purposes. People who work from home use mobile apps for video conferencing and communicating. Students use mobile apps to attend online classes which was the time where each student own their own phones. People watch more entertainment through streaming services and play games online. Mobile apps moved through many stages with high instant results. Moreover there were many mobile apps which created a space in people through trading, playing games online in a financial risk. Even an individual sitting at home started earning online in many ways. Mobile apps and mobile phones paved a way for new methods including social media marketing and E-commerce. Facing this pandemic was challenging but now imagining a world without Mobile phones is quite challenging. Social media marketing is the process of creating content and gaining website traffic that you have tailored to the context of each individual in social media platform in order to drive user engagement and sharing. There are some key social media terms like Hashtags and shares. Hashtags, shares are backbones to Social Media Platforms. They make your content easy for users to discover and therefore more likely that they’ll share it and yes shares are all that matters on social media. It also created many influencers, marketers, entrepreneurs. Though Social media has become a crucial part of doing business. Having an online presence is one of the best successes in this world. Each business and every brand has realized the importance of having an online presence. There raised a personal connection with their target audience. The pandemic ruined many lives and at the same time, it created a world where people started creating their own jobs for their survival. E-commerce was a booming solution born when pandemic spread .E-commerce started digging space in human minds where each and everything was through online. On-demand mobile apps are used to order food, groceries, vegetables, medicines, and an array of other items that can be safely left at the doorstep. The most exciting part was that there exists a huge competition where, it has seen a spike in website traffic mainly in the sectors like retail (groceries), healthcare, food, and entertainment, mostly driven by the global pandemic. You can sell your products or services online with an e-commerce business. E-commerce is one of the best options to be taken for making money from your home itself. The world is now rooted in Technology and Digital, you must think with brain to sustain your life. Covid-19 was an unexpected pandemic to our world. It created many thing the world ever witnessed. On other hand it can be said that it paved a way for new beginning where new inventions of apps and technologies helped the people to survive to a great extent. All our needs are now delivered easily through online. Plenty of the mobile app opportunities that arose in this crisis hinge on how the COVID-19 lockdowns impacted mobile users’ behaviour. Healthcare and Telemedicine app industry, Entertainment App industry, Education App Industry, Delivery App Industry and Social Networking App Industry was delivering higher outputs and mobile app development raised without limits. All these industries thrived and people started loving online presence. Mobile Game App witnessed many by introducing extra features to the games and this made people loving online games. These apps also featured some interesting facts like winning money by playing and this made every user to click once. There was a huge users to playing games online in this pandemic. Many spent their entire day by playing online games. The new updated features started making the audience thrilling and the way the mobile app industry flied needed to be a topic to take concern. And yes today, mobile apps or mobile phones became a necessity thing which was once an option for many. Time flies and people around the world is still fighting against the pandemic. All these development helped humans to sustain their life so far in this crucial situation. The fact is that the world is witnessing a huge crisis and the world is still surviving with developments in several industries. As the needs to survive increases, the option will change to necessities.
