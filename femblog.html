“A GIRL IS BORN WITH WINGS: SHE WILL FLY HIGH”

“Let the burning fire bloom in the patriarchal minds:”

-Woman are like match boxes which can burn out into fire within seconds.-

The question of equality between men and woman is as old as the human race. The subjugation of women is a central fact of history. Every new ideology is established only after a long struggle. Feminism is a wide range of movements and beliefs worldwide that vouch for equality between the sexes. Feminism is both an intellectual commitment and a political movement that seeks justice and equality for women and the end of sexism in all forms. Equal rights and Equal status coins with the ideology of feminism. Feminism is said to be an ideology which emphasizes the equality of gender and advocates maximization of women’s potential so that culturally and institutionally created constraints do not hinder the process and pattern of development amongst women. There is a popular misconception that feminism is an ideology which states that woman must be superior to men. But this is not what feminism actually signifies. It stands as an ideology that vouches for equality on all grounds. So feminism is not misandry, that is, hatred towards men, or women wanting to dominate over men in society. But beyond this, feminism aims to create a society where men are no longer conditioned in toxic masculinity. Feminism wants a society where a woman is seen as an individual before she is seen as a sexual object or the property of her household. Finally, feminism seek to break down the expectations that society holds on men to propagate toxic masculinity and patriarchy. Woman still have to face many challenges to overcome their problems on all grounds and so feminism as a movement aims to aid them by ending sexism, sexist exploitation and oppression, among other things. Every woman had her own dreams and desires but often, she has to bury them deep in the depths of heart because society views them as the secondary sex. The struggle for equality continues in the twenty-first century, notably the #MeeToo and Time’s up movements, which are considered part of the fourth wave of the feminist movement. Looking forward, woman will definitely light up their inner strength and, there will be a revolution where each female will empower and fly high beyond the reality. Remember, Woman will come up for the strongest battle and they will bloom as a fire around the toxic masculinity, hopefully.
